The idea

Cook a healthy fresh meal in 10 minutes. Fully automatically.

Raw ingredient in, fully cooked meal out — no washing, prepping, or cooking needed. Drop in the ingredients, walk away, and come back to a fresh cooked meal.

The machine cleans itself after each use so nothing cross-contaminates. It's the only kitchen appliance that handles the entire process from start to finish.

Engineering

Cook health-maxxing meals
with 0 effort.

01 — First Layer

Veggie & Meat Washer

Drop in raw ingredients. Each is washed thoroughly and automatically lowered to the second layer — no touching, no rinsing required.

02 — Second Layer

Chopper, Slicer & Stirrer

Veggies are chopped, meat is sliced, condiments are portioned and stirred. Everything then drops down to the cooking chamber below.

03 — Third Layer

Steamer & Cooker

Steam, stir-fry, and slow-cook — including rice and noodles. Your meal is ready 10 minutes after you put the ingredients in.

The entire machine is fully enclosed. After you take your meals out, each layer auto-washes itself so nothing ever cross-contaminates between uses.

The app

All you need to do is put in the ingredients.

The app reads your nutrient history and plans your meals. It shows you exactly what to put in and which compartment. You wait 10 minutes. That's it.

It tracks protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals over time. If you're low on Vitamin D it recommends salmon and sun-exposed mushrooms. No guessing, no research, no thinking.
